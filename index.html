Grunge History




What Is Grunge?

Grunge is a music genre beginning in the mid 1980s in the Seattle
and other towns nearby. Inspired by elements of punk rock and metal,
grunge is defined by its sound often described as "Sludgy" with its
use of a heavily distored electric guitar, intense drum beats and raspy
or gravelly vocals that vary in volume and tone that make this genre
of music quite unique.
