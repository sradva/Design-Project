Nirvana

Nirvana was formed in 1987 by Kurt Cobain(guitar and vocals) and Krist Novoselic(bass) over their time they had many drummers but the drummer they stuck with the most was Dave Grohl. Nirvana released their first album in 1989 titled "Bleach" without Grohl,he only joined a year later on their second and most popular album "Nevermind". Nevermind the ablum that brought them to fame includes some of their most popular song such as "Smells Like Teen Spirit", "Come as you are", "In bloom" and "Lithium". The Smells Like Teen Spirit music video was very successful on mtv in the early 90s and helped bring them to fame. The music video can be found on Youtube with over 1.7 billion views at the time of writing this. Nirvana's third and final album was In Utero, the most popular songs on the album being "Heart Shaped Box" and "All Apologies". Despite only releasing three studio albums Nirvana (and many other artists of the time) released MTV Unplugged in New York, a recorded live event where they played some of their songs on accoustic instraments instead of electric hence the name unplugged. Nirvana disbanded in April 1994 following the tragic suicide of gutairist/vocalist Kurt Cobain.

Pearl Jam

Pearl Jam was formed in 1990 by Stone Gossard(guitar), Jeff Ament(bass), Mike McCready(guitar), Eddie Vedder(vocals) and Dave Krusen(drums) who was later replaced by Dave Abbruzzese. Pearl Jam released their first album "Ten" in 1991. Ten contains some of Pearl Jam's most successful songs such as "Even Flow", "Alive", "Jeremy" and "Black". After "Ten" Pearl Jam released Like Nirvana, Pearl Jam also released an MTV Unplugged album. Unlike Nirvana, Pearl Jams MTV Unplugged contains some of their most popular songs that are found in ten.

Alice In Chains

Alice in Chains was formed in 1987 by Jerry Cantrell(guitar), Sean Kinney(drums), Mike Starr(bass) and Layne Staley(vocals). They released their first Ep "We Die Young" in July of 1990, later that same year they release their first album "Facelift" the top song of the album being "Man in a Box" which helped the album release the popularity that it did. In September of 1992, Alice in Chains released Dirt their second album. Dirt contained some of Alice in Chains most popular songs such as "Would?" , "Rooster", "Them Bones" aswell as "Down In A Hole". "Jar of Flies" releasing in 1993 was their 3 Ep consisted primarily of acoustic songs unlike their other works which consisted mostly of electric instruments. Jar of Flies is technically an album but was promoted as an Ep the most popular song on this Ep being Nutshell. Like the previous two bands Alice in Chains also had an MTV Unplugged album in 1996 and consisted of popular songs like "Nutshell","Down In A Hole", "Rooster" and "Would?"
